Power Consumption Visualization

Visualization of Power Consumption of a residence. Refrigerator, Stove/Oven, Dishwasher, Microwave, Washer + Dryer and the lighting power data of a house is visualized below. There are certain missing values and high values which are regarded as anomalies. We have left them as is in the vizualization since it is a more accurate representation of the data collected from the real world. The dataset used is provided by Dr. Patrick Mantey

How Different Groups Use Prescription Drugs

A large percentage of Americans have become dependent on prescription drugs to treat various medical conditions. If we analyze changes in drug use over the years with respect to the percentage of the population, we can see the overall health trend. We observed that drugs treating resperatory problems are more common for young people whereas the use of drugs treating blood and heart problems increase along with age.

Life of Sabina: what are the chances she will divorce?

This is the story of Sabina, an average woman from Kazakhstan (Sabina is a very popular Kazakh female name). Through her story we will get insight about the current mariatal situation in Kazakhstan. Why this is important? I believe, family is a building block of any society. Healthy families lead to healthy society. Unfortunately, the mariatal situation in Kazakhstan is getting worse every year. The purpose of this visualization is to inform people of Kazakhstan about this situation and let them know about the common reasons of the break-ups.

Public Transportation and Impact on Healthy Living: An Ethnic Divide?

TThe goal of the project is to enhance public health by providing visualizations to understand the correlation between public transportation availability and housing for different ethnicities in the community. Planning healthy communities and evaluating the impact of plans, projects, policy, and environmental changes on community health is crucial to create sustainable living spaces.
